Staten Island (cont'd)

This island is one of the five boroughs of New York City. It is separated from the rest of the city by the Narrows at the entrance to the harbor. The Staten Island Ferry connects the community of St. George at the northern tip of the island, to Whitehall Street at the Southern tip of Manhattan Island, a five mile journey across the center of the harbor.
